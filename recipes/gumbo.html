Gumbo

Home Page

Description

Gumbo is a cajun recipe found in many southern US states. It's very good!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup bacon drips
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 quarts water
  • 6 cubes beef bullion
  • 1 teaspoon cajun mix
  • canned vegetables

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, and bacon drippings.
  2. Chop all other vegetables, mix together.
  3. Stir vegetables and sausage into current mixture.
  4. Combine beef and water in pot and boil.
  5. Lower heat on pot, mix all ingredients and mixtures into pot.
  6. Mix in other meat and all other personally added ingredients.
